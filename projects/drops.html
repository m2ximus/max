Gamified Rewards

Drops

Pokemon Go, but the prizes are real. Brands drop cash, gift cards, and experiences at real-world locations. You find them.

About

Drops turns marketing into a game worth playing. Brands launch treasure hunts by dropping real rewards — cash, gift cards, products, exclusive experiences — at physical locations. Users discover them through proximity detection, AR, or QR scanning. It's a new way for brands to get people through the door, and a genuinely fun way for people to discover what's around them.

How it works
01

Brands create hunts

Set a budget, choose reward types, pick locations, and launch a treasure hunt in minutes through the brand dashboard.

02

Rewards get dropped

Cash, gift cards, products, free experiences — placed at real GPS coordinates, waiting to be found.

03

Users hunt in the real world

Open the map, spot nearby drops, walk to them. Claim through proximity, AR overlay, or scanning.

04

Everyone wins

Users get real prizes. Brands get real foot traffic and engagement data. No wasted impressions.
