Digital Legacy

Exit State

Your digital life deserves a plan. Crypto, passwords, online accounts — traditional wills weren't built for any of this.

About

Most wills cover the house, the car, the savings account. They don't cover your crypto wallets, your password vaults, your business subscriptions, or the 200 online accounts tied to your email. Exit State walks you through a guided process to catalogue everything — digital and physical — assign executors, allocate to beneficiaries, and generate a legally valid UK will. All through a brutalist interface that treats the subject with the seriousness it deserves.

How it works
01

Identity and executors

Start with who you are and who you trust. Designate the people responsible for carrying out your plan.

02

Catalogue everything

Crypto, property, financial accounts, digital assets, business holdings — add every asset type in one place.

03

Allocate to beneficiaries

Decide who gets what. Set percentages, specific items, or conditional allocations across all your assets.

04

Generate and export

Your will is generated as a legally formatted PDF. Add witnesses, sign, and you're covered.
