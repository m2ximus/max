AI Voice

Foan

Talk to anyone. Hear their real voice. A platform where fans have real-time voice conversations with AI clones of their favourite creators.

About

Creators can't talk to every fan. But what if their voice could? Foan lets creators train AI voice clones of themselves — their tone, their cadence, their personality — so fans can call and have a genuine conversation anytime. Creators earn per minute. Fans get 30 seconds free to test the connection, then pay to keep talking. 95 creator profiles already live with full dashboard controls for voice training, pricing, and earnings.

How it works
01

Creators train their voice

Upload voice samples through the creator dashboard. ElevenLabs builds a custom voice model matched to their real voice.

02

Fans browse and call

Discover creators, hear a 30-second free preview, and start a paid call with one tap. No downloads, no apps.

03

Real-time AI conversation

VAPI and LiveKit handle the call infrastructure. Low latency, natural flow, the creator's actual voice patterns.

04

Creators earn passively

Set your per-minute rate, track earnings in your dashboard, get paid via Stripe Connect. Revenue while you sleep.
