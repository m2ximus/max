Little Box

Donate where you are. Physical boxes around the world connect you to local causes. Scan, give, and watch your impact on the map.

About

Little Box puts a physical donation point in your path and connects it to the world. Walk past a box, scan the QR code, and give to a local cause in seconds. Every donation shows up on a live global map — an interactive 3D globe where you can see generosity happening in real time. Charities get a dashboard to track fundraising campaigns, manage their boxes, and export reports. Donors get leaderboards and the satisfaction of seeing exactly where their money went.

How it works
01

Find a box nearby

Physical Little Boxes placed at locations worldwide. Each one is linked to a local charity or cause.

02

Scan and give

Scan the QR code, pick an amount, and donate via Stripe. Takes less time than buying a coffee.

03

See your impact

Every donation lights up on a live 3D globe. Watch generosity ripple across the map in real time.

04

Charities track everything

Full dashboard with fundraising campaigns, box management, donor leaderboards, and exportable reports.
