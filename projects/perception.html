Perception

See what others can't. A marketplace where seekers post blind targets and perceivers submit intuitive insights — verified by AI.

About

Perception brings remote viewing out of the fringe and into a structured, verifiable platform. Seekers create research tasks with blind protocols — the perceiver never knows the target. They submit drawings, impressions, and descriptions. AI compares submissions against the actual target using image embeddings and pattern analysis. The best perceivers rise through a tier-based ranking system. Collectives can pool funds to crowdsource research into consciousness itself.

How it works
01

Seekers post blind targets

Create a research task with hidden target images and descriptions. Set the reward pool and wait for submissions.

02

Perceivers submit impressions

Without knowing the target, perceivers sketch, describe, and record their intuitive impressions through a guided session.

03

AI analyses accuracy

Google Gemini and CLIP embeddings compare submissions to targets. Objective scoring replaces subjective judgement.

04

Rankings and collectives

Top perceivers climb tiers. Collectives pool funding for larger experiments. Wearable integration tracks biometric data during sessions.
