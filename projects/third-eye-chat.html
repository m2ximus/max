Social Experiment

Third Eye Chat

Can you read between the blur? An anonymous chat where messages are intentionally blurred — and the best conversations are verified on-chain.

About

What if you could prove you understood someone without reading their words? Third Eye Chat pairs strangers in a blind conversation where every message is blurred beyond recognition. You type. They type. Neither of you can read the other's words clearly — but the system analyses coherence between your messages in real time. The most coherent conversations rise to "The Hill," a public leaderboard. Every ranked conversation is cryptographically hashed and anchored on Base L2. You can't fake a connection.

How it works
01

Connect with a stranger

No profiles, no photos. You're matched anonymously and dropped into a chat where the rules are different.

02

Messages are blurred

Every message you send and receive is intentionally blurred. You have to feel the conversation, not read it.

03

AI measures coherence

OpenAI analyses the pattern and flow between blurred messages. Are you actually in sync, or just guessing?

04

Verified on-chain

Top conversations are hashed and anchored on Base L2. Cryptographic proof that the connection was real.
