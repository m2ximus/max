AI Planner

Together

Never waste another weekend. AI fills your calendar with high-energy trips matched to your vibe, location, and budget.

About

Most weekends go to waste because planning feels like work. Together fixes that. Give it your age, where you live, how much energy you have, and what you can spend — and it generates a full calendar of weekend trips. Each one exportable to iCal with a single tap. No more "what should we do this weekend?"

How it works
01

Share your details

Age, location, how far you're willing to travel, and what kind of energy you're bringing.

02

Pick your vibe

Chill beach day or all-night adventure? Set the tone and let AI handle the specifics.

03

Get a full calendar

Not just one trip — a whole calendar of weekends planned out, so you always have something to look forward to.

04

Export and go

Each trip exports as an ICS file. Add it to your calendar and stop overthinking.
