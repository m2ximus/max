AI Travel

Trip

Your next trip, planned in under a minute. Answer a few questions and AI builds your perfect day-by-day itinerary.

About

Trip takes the stress out of travel planning. Tell it where you're going, when, who you're with, your vibe, your budget, and what you love doing. In under sixty seconds, it hands you a detailed daily itinerary you can export straight to your calendar. No more spreadsheets. No more 47 open tabs.

How it works
01

Tell us the basics

Destination, dates, group type — a quick wizard gathers everything the AI needs in a few taps.

02

Set your vibe

Adventure or relaxation? Street food or fine dining? Your preferences shape every recommendation.

03

AI builds your plan

Google Gemini generates a day-by-day itinerary tailored to your style, budget, and interests.

04

Export to calendar

One click and your entire trip lives in your calendar as individual events. Ready to go.
